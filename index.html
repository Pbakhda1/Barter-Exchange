Rules & safety

Build trust and keep the platform clean.

Allowed

  • Legal services and items
  • Clear value expectations
  • House swaps with written rules
  • Platonic companionship only (community activities)

Not allowed

  • Sexual services or escorting
  • Adult content listings
  • Illegal goods or unsafe offers
  • Harassment, coercion, or exploitation

Safety tips

  • Meet in public places for item trades
  • Use ID verification for high value trades
  • Document the agreement (simple checklist)
  • For house swaps: deposits, photos, rules, and dates
House swap note: Real house swaps should include identity verification, contracts, insurance checks, and clear cancellation/refund rules.
