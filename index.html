FAQ

Common questions about bartering and safety.

What can I barter?

Anything legal: services (mowing, cleaning, painting, handyman), and items (tools, furniture, electronics, etc.).

How do you decide “equal value”?

Users estimate value. A real app could use price suggestions, receipts, and community ratings to improve fairness.

How do I stay safe?

Meet in public places, verify profiles, use in-app messaging, and consider identity verification for high-value trades.

Can I barter service hours?

Yes. You can list service hours or bundle services + items to meet the value.
